Professional Mission

My goal as a professional is to contribute to the wellbeing and longevity of society by working to promote a more just and more sustainable economy. Whether working to shed light on and guide intervention in the unjust concentration of environmental risks on the most vulnerable communities, working to plan more livable, decarbonized cities, or doing analyses to guide clean energy development, it is my hope to leave the world better than I found it.

Personal Philosophy

The people who live now have no business in mortgaging the future to temporarily enrich the present. This makes it even more egregious that the future is, indeed, presently being mortgaged away for the sake of enriching an ultra-minority of those who currently live. Even if everyone currently living benefited equally from our present unsustainability, it would be indefensible to willfully perpetuate the unsustainability beyond what is presently unavoidable. As it stands, only a very small group stands to benefit even temporarily from slowing the push towards a greener economy. As such, we should consider why it is that their voices are so much louder than everyone else's that we're still on that course.
