Navigating Pierce County

With the help of this simple map, it's easy to route anywhere you please within Pierce County Washington. To get started, just click where you are right now and select 'Start from this location', then click where you want to go and select 'Go to this location'. Once you've done that, the route you should take will be automatically determined and displayed in a sidebar to the right. Misclicked one of those times? Just click again in the right spot and select the correct option again. Why only Pierce County (and some tacked on bits around the edges)? Well, if it were a useful routing map, that would pose a potential problem for my mapbox account if this got out, so it's deliberately not useful as a competitor to the mainstream routing maps.

Personal Philosophy

Every ton of carbon we release into the atmosphere and every kilogram of metal we refine then dump into a landfill during our lifetimes represents something taken from the future for our own benefit. We, as those currently alive, have an affirmative duty to those who come after us to restrain these takings to only that which is to sustainable, and to give back as much as we take in whatever way achievable. Doing otherwise is akin to mortgaging our collective future as a species with no repayment plan, in the name of immediate gratification and expedience.
