Time and Place - A Geolocation Demonstration

If you press the white button on the left with a black dot in it, this map will request permission to geolocate the device you are accessing it from. This information will be used to zoom in on that estimated location, to provide an estimate of the level of accuracy regarding that estimate (a red circle), and to determine whether or not the sun is currently in the sky there to determine if a bright or dark map is better (if you disagree with it, you can use the control in the top right to manually reselect the color of the map). Please press the button only once, unless you move a substantial distance between clicks. This information will not be stored nor delivered to any outside party.

Personal Philosophy

Every ton of carbon we release into the atmosphere and every kilogram of metal we refine then dump into a landfill during our lifetimes represents something taken from the future for our own benefit. We, as those currently alive, have an affirmative duty to those who come after us to restrain these takings to only that which is to sustainable, and to give back as much as we take in whatever way achievable. Doing otherwise is akin to mortgaging our collective future as a species with no repayment plan, in the name of immediate gratification and expedience.
